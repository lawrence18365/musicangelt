Cookies Policy

Last updated: 15 May 2026

We use a small number of cookies and similar technologies. None of them are advertising cookies. This page lists exactly what's set and why.

Consent

When you first visit the site, no analytics cookies are set. We use Google Consent Mode v2 in default-deny mode, meaning analytics storage is denied until you explicitly accept on our cookie banner. You can change your mind at any time by clearing your browser's storage for this site.

What we set if you accept

NameProviderPurposeExpiry
_ga Google Analytics Distinguish unique visitors anonymously 13 months
_ga_WV874YXC8Z Google Analytics Persist session state for our GA4 property 13 months

What we set regardless

NameProviderPurposeExpiry
mc:consent:v1 (localStorage) MusicAngel Remember whether you accepted or declined cookies, so we don't ask every visit Until you clear browser storage

This is a strictly-necessary item used to honour your consent choice. It does not track you.

Changing your mind

Clear localStorage for musicangel.ie in your browser (DevTools → Application → Local Storage) and reload — the banner returns.

## Diagnosis

- Bad geography: partial. `wedding bands north west ireland` served in Dublin, and some competitor county queries leaked in.
- Thin sample: yes. Most non-Cork counties have 1-13 clicks only.
- Low intent: not mostly. Most clicked terms are direct county wedding-band searches.
- Poor landing-page fit: not proven. County terms map to county pages, but D1 leads often submit on band pages after browsing.
- Too broad match: partial. Leakage comes from phrase close variants, not broad expansion.
- Bad query mix: partial. Competitor leakage should be controlled, but not enough to pause County.

Rule: if County spends another EUR 40-50 from today without a verified D1 paid lead, move EUR 1-2/day from County into Brand & Bands first, then Wedding Bands Ireland. Do not increase total account budget.

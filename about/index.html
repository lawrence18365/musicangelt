About MusicAngel

MusicAngel is a small Irish wedding-music platform built around four live wedding bands. Here's who we are, what each band stands for, and how the booking process actually works.

What MusicAngel is

MusicAngel is a small Irish wedding-music platform built around four live wedding bands — The Beat Boutique, Sway Social, The Best Men, and Blacktye. Every band plays 100% live, no backing tracks. Every band plays all of Ireland.

The idea is simple: instead of trawling forty wedding-band websites and trying to compare them, you visit one place, see four hand-picked acts side by side, and pick the right fit for your day.

The four bands

Each band has a distinct identity. They're not interchangeable acts. They were picked because they each serve a different kind of wedding:

  • The Beat Boutique — Old Hollywood glamour meets modern electricity. Six-decade repertoire, 233+ songs, every note played live.
  • Sway Social — Four lead vocalists, no backing tracks, non-stop dance-floor energy. Award-winning. Eurovision-tested.
  • The Best Men — Big vocals, tight harmonies, feel-good and packed dance floor.
  • Blacktye — Sleek, polished arrangements that build from elegant reception tempo into full-party energy.

See the side-by-side comparison if you're trying to figure out which one fits your day.

How booking works

  1. Send an enquiry on the main form with your date, venue, and a preferred band (or "not sure — recommend"). Takes under two minutes.
  2. You'll get a reply within one working day — typically much faster — with availability and a tailored quote.
  3. If you're happy, we'll send a simple booking contract. A deposit secures the date.
  4. 4-6 weeks before the wedding we finalise the setlist, first-dance song, and timings.
  5. On the day, the band arrives 2 hours before they're due to start. They play. Your guests are very, very glad they came.

The standards all four bands meet

  • 100% live performance. No backing tracks. Every harmony, every solo, every fill played in the room.
  • One new song learned for every couple. Usually the first dance. Included in every package.
  • DJ-style music between and after live sets. Played through the band's own PA — no audible gap when the band stops.
  • Full PA and stage lighting. Sized to the venue. Confirmed with the venue's events team ahead of the day.
  • Public liability insurance. Standard for any reputable Irish wedding band — required by most venues.
  • Established backup plans. If a band member is ill, there's a tested dep ready.

Where the bands play

All of Ireland. North to south, east to west. The four bands have collectively played at most of Ireland's well-known wedding venues — from Ashford Castle to Adare Manor to Cliff House Hotel. See the venues directory for the full list of venues we have bespoke pages for. If your venue isn't there, we still play it — just mention it when you enquire.

Pricing

Packages start from €2,800. The final quote depends on date, venue location, ceremony or drinks-reception add-ons, and the package chosen. Full breakdown in our 2026 pricing guide.

Why MusicAngel, not just one of the bands directly

Two reasons:

  1. Comparison. The "perfect" wedding band depends on the kind of wedding you're throwing. We've thought carefully about which of the four bands suits which kind of day. Talking to MusicAngel directly means honest recommendations across all four, rather than each band telling you they're the right one.
  2. Coverage. If your first-choice band isn't available on your date, we can immediately offer the next-best fit without you starting the search over.
"The right wedding band is the one that fits your venue, your guest list, and your night — not the one with the loudest marketing. We do that match-making for you."

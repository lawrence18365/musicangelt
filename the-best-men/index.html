About the band

Big vocals, proven experience, full dance floor

The Best Men have been performing together since 2000, with all four members working as full-time musicians. That experience shows in the way they hold a mixed-generation wedding floor.

The band is a four-person group covering vocals, drums, bass, guitar and keyboard. They perform 100% live, bring their own sound and lighting, and build the night around big vocals, strong harmonies, medleys and crowd reads rather than a flat run-through of songs.

As with all our bands, our full-time booking coordinator handles the detail before the wedding. That makes The Best Men especially strong for couples who want high-energy performance and a booking process that feels organised from the first enquiry.

Questions answered

How much does The Best Men cost for a wedding in Ireland?

Starting from €2450. The final quote depends on your wedding date, venue location, ceremony or drinks-reception add-ons, and the package you choose. Send an enquiry with your date and venue and we'll come back with a full quote within one working day.

How long do the band play for?

A standard evening package is 2.5 hours (including the 15 minute break around halfway through) with 2 hours DJ service afterwards. Ceremony music and drinks reception sets can be added. Just mention what you need in your enquiry.

Do The Best Men bring their own sound and lighting?

Yes. The band travel with a full PA and stage lighting suited to most Irish wedding venues. For larger venues we'll confirm any additional production needs with you and the venue ahead of the day.

Can we request specific songs, including our first dance?

Yes, especially for your First Dance. If you have a couple of songs you would like the band to play, or a couple you would rather not hear please do let us know. The band learn one new song specifically for the couple as part of the package, and read the room to play what the crowd wants.

Where in Ireland do you play?

All of Ireland, including Dublin, Cork, Galway, Kerry, Limerick, Mayo, Wicklow, Donegal and beyond. Travel may affect the quote in remote areas; mention your venue when enquiring.

How do we book?

A booking fee secures the date.

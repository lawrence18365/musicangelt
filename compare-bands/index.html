Which band suits your wedding?

If you want maximum dance-floor energy

Book Sway Social. Four musicians, four voices, live guitar, bass, keys and drums, with a set built for fast transitions and a full floor.

If your guest list spans generations

Book The Beat Boutique. Their 200+ song repertoire covers first dances, standards, rock, soul, disco, dance anthems, Irish favourites and waltzes.

If your venue is small and intimate

Book Blacktye. Their packages cover ceremony, drinks duo, wedding band, band and DJ, plus DJ and sax options.

If you want a warm, feel-good party

Book The Best Men. They have been performing together since 2000, with big vocals, medleys and a full-time booking coordinator.

If your venue is Adare Manor, Ballyfin, or Cliff House

Book Blacktye or The Beat Boutique. Blacktye brings the broadest full-day package range, while The Beat Boutique brings the fixed four-piece line-up and deep repertoire.

If your venue is Powerscourt, Mount Falcon, or a marquee

Book Sway Social or The Best Men. Both built for marquee acoustics and confident, packed dance floors.

If you can't decide between two

Send an enquiry mentioning both. We'll come back with the closest fit based on your venue, date and guest count, and you can always make the call after a showcase. See our showcases guide.

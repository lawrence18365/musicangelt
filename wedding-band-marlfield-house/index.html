Before you book

What matters for a band at Marlfield House

A wedding band quote for Marlfield House should be based on the actual reception room, guest count, finish time, and whether you need ceremony or drinks-reception music before the evening set. For a Regency country house, exclusive-use option with a Wexford-classical, boutique-luxury feel, the wrong band can look underpowered even if the song list is good.

  • Room and guest count: tell us which room you are using and roughly how many guests are attending, so the PA and stage footprint are right.
  • Timeline: confirm speeches, first dance time, band start time, and whether the venue needs the band loaded in before guests enter the room.
  • Music brief: share three songs you love and three you do not want. That helps us understand the live-music priorities for your day.
  • Extras: ceremony music, drinks-reception sets, sax, or a later DJ finish should be quoted upfront rather than added late.

Couples often ask

Questions answered

What's the best wedding band for Marlfield House?

Blacktye and The Beat Boutique are the most common Marlfield picks, both polished, both 100% live.

Is the Marlfield Conservatory suited to a full band?

Yes, the room accommodates a 4-piece with PA and stage lighting.

Does Marlfield offer exclusive use?

Yes, exclusive-use bookings are available and recommended for larger weddings. Confirm with the venue's events team.

How far in advance to book?

12-18 months for peak Saturdays; earlier for exclusive-use weekend bookings.

Corporate Events & Christmas Parties

The same four bands that play Irish weddings also play corporate events. Christmas parties, gala dinners, product launches, awards nights, summer parties, conferences. Different brief, same standard. 100% live, no backing tracks.

Corporate event types we cover

  • Christmas parties. December slots book 6+ months in advance. Both hotel ballrooms and bespoke venue setups.
  • Gala dinners & black-tie events. Where the brief is "elegant during dinner, full party after speeches."
  • Awards nights. Walk-on music, between-award music, post-ceremony live set.
  • Product launches. Polished, brand-aligned music. Live performance reads better in launch videos than DJ playlists.
  • Summer parties. Outdoor marquee receptions for tech companies, professional services, financial firms.
  • Conferences & off-sites. Closing-night party at multi-day corporate events.
  • Private events. Milestone birthdays, anniversaries, retirements, fundraising galas.

What's different from a wedding booking?

  • Brand alignment. We work with your events team or agency to match the band's energy to the brand's voice. Some corporates want the band visible and on-brand; others want them dressed neutrally.
  • Setlist tailoring. Less first-dance, more crowd-energy. Some corporates ask for branded song moments (your CEO's favourite song to open the evening, etc.).
  • Invoicing. Corporate bookings are invoiced through standard B2B terms (Net 30 typical). VAT receipts included.
  • Insurance. Full public liability cover included; PL/Indemnity certificates provided for venue/legal teams.
  • Sound restrictions. Corporate venues often have stricter decibel limits, and the band plans to fit within them.

Pricing

Corporate events typically start higher than wedding pricing because of: bespoke setlist work, branded production requests, B2B invoicing overhead, and the premium hours corporate events tend to fall on (Friday evenings, Christmas Saturdays).

Indicative range: €3,500-€7,000+ depending on band, venue, hours, and any production/branding extras. A tailored quote follows your enquiry.

Which band suits a corporate event?

  • The Beat Boutique: boutique polish that aligns with luxury-brand events. Six-decade repertoire works across mixed-age corporate crowds.
  • Blacktye. Sleek, considered musicianship for events where guests will notice the detail.
  • Sway Social. Four lead vocalists, non-stop energy, and the right pick for a corporate Christmas party where the brief is "keep them on the floor."
  • The Best Men. Feel-good, harmony-led, and works for summer parties and informal corporate events.

Christmas party availability

December books fast. Mid-December Saturdays go 6-9 months ahead. If you're planning a corporate Christmas party, enquire as early as possible, at minimum 4 months ahead for the major Saturdays.

Frequently asked questions

Do MusicAngel bands play corporate events?

Yes. All four bands play weddings and corporate events. The format differs: different setlist focus, branded production options, and B2B invoicing, but the live-performance standard is the same.

How much does a corporate event live band cost in Ireland?

Indicative pricing starts from €3,500 for a 4-piece live corporate event package, depending on band, venue, hours, and any branded production extras. Christmas-party Saturdays in December and corporate awards nights tend toward the higher end of the range.

How early should we book a band for a corporate Christmas party?

Mid-December Saturdays book 6-9 months ahead. Earlier is better. December is the most-competitive month of the year for live-band bookings.

Can the band match our event's brand or theme?

Yes. Branded production (lighting colours, dress code, intro song, branded screens during the set) can be arranged. Discuss the brief when you enquire.

Do you provide VAT-receipted invoices?

Yes. Corporate bookings are invoiced through standard B2B terms with VAT receipts. Net 30 payment is standard. Public liability and indemnity certificates are provided.

How to request a specific song

When you book, you'll have a chance to share a list of must-plays and a do-not-play list. Most couples send 5-10 must-plays and 2-3 do-not-plays. The rest is the band reading the room, which is the whole reason you book a real live act.

What if the song isn't in our repertoire?

Every MusicAngel package includes one new song learned specifically for the couple, typically the first dance. If you have a specific must-have song beyond that, ask early. Some bands will learn additional new songs as a paid extra.

Frequently asked questions

What's the full wedding band song list?

The combined MusicAngel repertoire across the four bands spans six decades and 400+ songs. This page shows the cross-section, the ~80 songs that consistently land at Irish weddings. Each band has their own deeper specialist repertoire on top.

Can we request a specific song?

Yes. Every package includes one new song learned for the couple, typically the first dance. Additional song requests beyond that can be added for a small extra charge. Send must-plays and do-not-plays with your enquiry.

Will the band play traditional Irish songs?

Yes. Galway Girl, Tell Me Ma, Whiskey in the Jar, The Fields of Athenry, and other Irish singalongs are part of the late-night repertoire across all four bands.
